What is CollectibleFind?

CollectibleFind is the #1 free directory of collectibles influencers and content creators. We cover 15 categories — sports cards, trading cards & TCG, coins & currency, stamps & philately, toys & figurines, comics & books, sports memorabilia, Hollywood & entertainment, sneakers & streetwear, vintage & antiques, paper ephemera, niche & emerging — and more.

Our mission is simple: help collectors find the best educators, creators, and influencers in their niche. Whether you're just getting started or a seasoned collector, CollectibleFind helps you discover the people who will teach you, entertain you, and connect you with your community.

How We Work

Every profile in the CollectibleFind directory is manually reviewed by our team before going live. We verify platform links, confirm the creator is actively producing content, and write or review each bio for accuracy. We do not accept payment for rankings — all standard listings are free and ordered by audience size and content quality.

Creators can submit a profile for free using our Get Listed form. Approved profiles typically appear within 3–5 business days.
